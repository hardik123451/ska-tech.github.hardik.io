About Us

Our SKA Technology

Ska Technology, which is proudly located in the thriving centre of New Delhi, has been a light of excellence in the networking and cabling services business since its founding in 2007. We have developed a reputable reputation with more than a decade of unrelenting dedication, known for accuracy and dependability.

CISCO Manageable Switch

Modern UPS services, cutting-edge networking and cabling solutions, and a broad range of electrical expertise, including installation, power distribution, energy-saving solutions, and cutting-edge security systems, are just a few of the services we offer. We at Ska Technology are committed to enlightening you and giving you control over the future.

The Mission

SKA Technology believes that the success of any individual or a group depends on professionalism in work, time management and quality of workmanship. With commitment, dedication and loyalty, it now aims at widening its scope of work and creating a comfortable, stress-free environment.

Cable Dressing

SKA Technology was established in the year 1998 to provide complete solution in computer LAN Cabling (UTP & Optical Fiber) all type of electrical works, Later on it also started work on installations, operation, maintenance of DG sets and AMF panel.

Security and Access Control Systems

Access control is a data security process that enables organizations to manage who is authorized to access corporate data and resources. Secure access control uses policies that verify users are who they claim to be and ensures appropriate control access levels are granted to users.

Fire Safety Solutions

We provide Fire safety solutions is the set of tools to reduce destruction caused by fire, like fire-alarms, fire-alert-system, Fire sensors, Safety Cylinders, etc. Fire safety measures include those that are intended to prevent the ignition of an uncontrolled fire and those that are used to limit the spread and impact of a fire.
